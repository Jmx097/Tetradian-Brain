# Analysis: Patreon launch

## Executive Summary
Tom Graves introduces his suite of tools for managing change, currently aimed at enterprise specialists, and announces a Patreon launch to fund their redesign for broader accessibility to anyone handling change. The goal is to create open, freely available resources like step-by-step processes, diagrams, and worksheets through community collaboration.

## Key Takeaways
- Tom Graves develops tools, worksheets, and resources to provide a unified view of change and aid decision-making across all scales, from individual businesses to community efforts.
- Current tools target specialists like enterprise architects and strategists, but the Patreon initiative seeks to redesign them for universal use.
- Funding via Patreon will support extensive redesign, rethinking, and collaboration to produce easy-to-use materials like fire diagrams, worksheets, and worked examples.
- The aim is to make all tools openly and freely available to everyone dealing with any kind of change.

## Actionable Insights / Tutorials
- Join Patreon to contribute to the redesign process and help build step-by-step processes, easy-to-use diagrams, worksheets, and worked examples.
- Collaborate with Tom Graves and others to adapt the tools for non-specialists, expanding from enterprise use to everyday applications.
